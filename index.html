What Maketh a Banger?

A screed by Becca.

Humanity has been banging for eons, certainly. Yet a banger itself, it's atomic constitution, it's viscera, it's esoteric vibe…what maketh a banger? Tis but a simple query. For it is not if or under which circumstances one may bang, nay, a common mistake. What maketh a banger?

It is the cause and the effect. The quid and the pro quo. The cat and, indeed, the bag it is let out of. To be a banger is no feat for small insignificant tracks. It is not only putting one's hands up, it must also be the declaration that they are playing one's song. It is not only the repeated supplication of if one has to, it is the bewilderment that one besought to linger.

How can such esoteric provocations be defined in the modern zeitgeist? The luddites will say they cannot be, yet the educated human will disagree vigorously. Any human experience such as banger reception can be definitively qualified.

Ask yourself, human, if you have experienced any of the following symptoms associated with bangers:

  1. An incandescent wave of warmth spreading outwards from the point beneath your nose
  2. Your human flesh hand flexing into a claw that clasps without warning upon the shoulder of a colleague
  3. A primal vocalisation of “ohh, hell yeah”
  4. A curiously robust urge to make out with a rando
An affirmative answer to two of the above unequivocally constitutes exposure to a certified banger. Be joyous! Be free!

One cannot help but be reminded of the immortal maxim of Bruce of New Jersey: “I'm on fire”. Such powerful words from The Boss. May it bring you inconceivable delight to revive these bangers, these tokens of infamy, these gods of old, these unruly satyrs. Use them to slight your enemies, betray your better selves, and most importantly, to make out with randos.
